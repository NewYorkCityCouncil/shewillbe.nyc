About the Initiative…

Speaker Melissa Mark-Viverito & New York City Council are introducing . . . There's something special about the young women of New York City that makes them different from all other young women in the nation. Their tenacity, their strength and their passion push them to fight for their dreams. They are fierce students, sisters, mothers, friends and New Yorkers. Yet, young women of color have been denied the opportunity for advancement and success due to pervasive and systemic disparity. No more. 

It's time you, the young women of this great city, to tell us who you will be when you have everything you need to succeed. Whether is healthcare, education, equal pay for equal work or just life, we want to know who you are and we want to hear your story. What type of policy or legislation would you create to help the young women of New York? What resources do you need? What advice would you give to other young women? How can we better serve YOU?
